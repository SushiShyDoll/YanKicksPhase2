How to Measure

To find your perfect size, measure your foot length from heel to toe while standing. Use the size chart below to find your corresponding size.

Size Chart

US Size EU Size Foot Length (inches)
7 40 9.5

Fit Tips

  • Measure your feet at the end of the day when they're at their largest
  • Measure both feet and use the larger measurement
  • Leave about a thumb's width of space at the toe
  • If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up
